## 2. Why Traditional Transformations Fail

**Headline stat:** 70–80% failure rate for traditional finance transformations.

**Failure modes:**
- Cost focus only (ignore strategic value)
- Big upfront commitment (no proof first)
- Underestimate data quality issues
- Ignore organizational change complexity
- Measure activity, not business outcomes
- 24-month programs with no early wins

**Key message:** "We've designed our approach to address every failure mode."
